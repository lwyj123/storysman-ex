^Welcome!
This is a simple graphical MUD developed for the
Enter The Mud code jam on itch.io.

^Map
≡ means stuff is on the ground
∆ means bad guys are hanging out
≜ means there are bad guys and stuff
* is a player, middle one is you

^Controls
%Cursor keys: Move
%/: Open command mode (nonfunctional)
%!: Open chat mode
%Esc: Toggle sticky chat
%Ctrl-C: Quit
%Tab: Toggle log/inventory panel view

Other keys are labelled in the UI.

^❦
